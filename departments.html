Department of Computer Science

Offers courses in programming, algorithms, data structures, and artificial intelligence. The department has a dedicated lab with high-performance computers.

Department of Physics

Focuses on theoretical and experimental physics. Research includes topics in quantum mechanics, electromagnetism, and astrophysics.

Department of Business Administration

Provides courses in management, marketing, finance, and human resources. It prepares students for leadership roles in the business world.

Department of Arts

Includes programs in history, philosophy, and literature, offering a comprehensive understanding of human culture and society.
